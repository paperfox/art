Upcoming Events

Gallery 01760 Group Show

For the month of October, I'll have a piece in a group show at Gallery 01760 on Adams St in Natick. The opening is September 30th, 6pm - 7pm. Sadly, I can't be there but my fellow artists will be and it will be a great time!

Natick Artist's Open Studio

I'll be at Open Studios again this year. You'll find me at the First Congregational Church in downtown Natick with some other awesome Natick Art Association artists. Come visit us October 18 and 19, between 11am and 5pm.

Snowfarm Seconds Sale

My art, along with many other amazing artists, will be available at the Snowfarm Seconds Sale happening the last 3 weekends in November. More details here

Other than specific events, October is always a busy art month for me. I've been sketching away at ideas for this year's prompt list. For the last several years I've followed Stephanie Law's Undying Tales prompt list. I love the mix of mythology and drawing attention to endangered species. Feel free to draw along, or just follow the fun on instagram.

A few unrelated ideas snuck into my fall sketching as well...
