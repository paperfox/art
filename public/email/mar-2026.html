Upcoming Events

Natick Farmers' Market, Winter Market

Saturday, March 7, 9am to 1pm - I'll be at the Farmers' market in Natick next Saturday. Stop by and say hi! I'll be downstairs with the other crafters and bread. I can personally endorse both baked goods vendors and the candied nuts vendors as I've been a committed patron over the last few months.

If you've never been to the Winter market, it's at the Common Street Spiritual Center, right next to the park where the outdoor market is usually held.
13 Common St, Natick, MA 01760

Art in Bloom

May 2 and 3 - My recently completed work 'Cycles' has been accepted for the Art in Bloom show at the Morse Institute Library. This is a collaboration between Natick Art Association, the library, and the Garden club. Books are selected which then inspire art pieces which in turn are the inspiration for floral arrangements. This all culminates in a really fantastic exhibition of all 3 together.

Art on the Common

May 9th, 9am to 1pm - Once the Natick Farmers' Market gets back outside on the common, you can find me at Art on the Common as well, along with other amazing Natick Art Association artists.

I've been painting a lot of small scale works of clouds lately, both to study the infinite forms they can take, and to make sure each piece can be finished in the bits of time I'm able to carve out in my week. Clouds, to me, feel like freedom and possibilities. And lately, it feels like we all need both.

A preview of just a few of the clouds I've painted recently. I'll have these and more with me at the farmers' market Saturday!

Here's to hoping for less snow and some real spring weather soon!
