What's New and Happening

Hi there!

2025 has been a blast! New art, new markets, new events, and of course, meeting you all!

Upcoming In 2026

Nothing finalized yet for 2026 here's a peek at the plan for next year:

Winter Market

Natick Farmers Market runs an indoor market all winter. I don't have a date yet, but I'll send a note when I have a date of the one I'll be at.

Online Shop

I've been poking at setting up an online shop and next year will be the year to make it happen - wish me luck! More updates to come.

Art in the Park and Natick Artist Open Studios

Of course I'll be back in 2026! It was so much fun this year - I hope to see you again.

Snow Farm Courses

2026 classes are posted, and I'm eyeing which one I'll be taking. I'm not teaching but some of my favorite teachers are! See Snow Farm 2026 Class List

Taking a chance to reflect as the year comes to a close, there's a lot of things I'm proud of in 2025. I've made many pieces that I love. I've gotten more involved with Natick Art Association, and through them, more involved with the community as a whole. It brings me a lot of joy to bring art into the community and hopefully inspire more artists of all ages. Let's all get out there and get creative together!

Unrelated to all things art, the last month has been particularly busy. We adopted a second dog! He's a gem and we're all so happy to have him!

Happy Holidays and Happy New Year! Catch you in 2026!
