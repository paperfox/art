Upcoming Events

  • Sweetwater Coffee & Tea Show

    Now - May 31st - Natick Art Association group show is up and awesome at Sweetwater. Stop by and see my and my association member friends' works while grabbing a tasty drink and a munchy.

  • Post Road Art Center Exhibition

    April 20th - May 18th - My art has been accepted into the juried exhibition at Post Road Art Center, "Spring Forward".Swing by and check it out, many amazing works are there and and it's a great place to pick up your art supplies too.
    1 Boston Post Rd. E, Marlborough, MA 01752

  • Art in Bloom

    May 2 and 3 - My recently completed work 'Cycles' has been accepted for the Art in Bloom show at the Morse Institute Library. This is a collaboration between Natick Art Association, the library, and the Garden club. Books are selected which then inspire art pieces which in turn are the inspiration for floral arrangements. This all culminates in a really fantastic exhibition of all 3 together.

  • Art on the Common

    May 16th, 9am to 1pm - Once the Natick Farmers' Market gets back outside on the common, you can find me at Art on the Common as well, along with other amazing Natick Art Association artists.
